In a bowl take butter, sugar flour, cocoa powder and mix
till it becomes a thick paste
add vanilla essence, baking soda and baking powder 
mix thoughly
put the batter in a cake mould covered with baking paper, occasionaly adding chocolate chunks
place the cake mould in a preheated oven/microwave
leave it for 30 to 40 minutes
test it with a toothpick
if not then place it back for another 5 to 10 minutes
if ready let the cake cool or it might break
slowly take the cake out of the cake mould and decorate it with remaining chocolate chunks
